Our Angeles City Campus

Nestled in the vibrant and historic heart of Angeles City, Lunarity University is committed to brilliance and discovery. Our location in Pampanga, the Culinary Capital of the Philippines, inspires our students to embrace innovation and resilience.

We combine cutting-edge curriculum with deep respect for Kapampangan heritage. Our faculty fosters an environment where every student can achieve their full potential, illuminating their path to becoming leaders in their chosen fields.
